## Welcome to Skillment

Skillment is a comprehensive assessment and event management platform designed for organizations that need to conduct secure, scalable, and insightful evaluations. Whether you're running technical interviews, certification exams, or large-scale assessments, Skillment provides the tools you need to succeed.

Ready to dive in? Here's how to get started with Skillment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Organization">

  </Step>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Candidates">
