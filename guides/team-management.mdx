## Overview

Skillment's team management features allow you to collaborate effectively with colleagues, assign roles, and maintain organized workflows across your assessment programs.

## Adding Team Members

### Invite New Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Team Settings">
    Go to **Settings** â†’ **Team Management** in your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitations">
    Click "Invite Team Member" and enter:
    - **Email Address**
    - **Role** (Admin, Manager, or Viewer)
    - **Departments** (optional)
    - **Welcome Message** (optional)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Permissions">
    Configure what the team member can access:
    - Assessment creation and editing
    - Participant management
    - Results viewing
    - Organization settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Team Roles & Permissions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Organization Admin" icon="crown">
    **Full Access**: All features, billing, team management, organization settings
    - Create and manage all assessments
    - Add/remove team members
    - Access billing and subscription settings
    - Configure organization-wide policies
    - Export all data and reports
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Assessment Manager" icon="user-tie">
    **Assessment Management**: Create assessments, manage participants, view results
    - Create and edit assessments
    - Manage participants and invitations
    - View and export assessment results
    - Configure proctoring settings
    - Cannot modify team or billing settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Assessment Viewer" icon="eye">
    **Read-Only Access**: View assessments and results only
    - View assessment details and questions
    - See participant lists and progress
    - Access results and analytics
    - Export reports (with limitations)
    - Cannot create or modify assessments
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Department Lead" icon="users">
    **Department-Specific Access**: Manage assessments within assigned departments
    - Full access to department assessments
    - Manage department participants
    - View department analytics
    - Cannot access other departments
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Set up assessment approval processes:

1. **Creator** builds the assessment
2. **Reviewer** validates content and settings
3. **Approver** gives final approval to launch
4. **Publisher** sends invitations to participants

## Security & Access Control

### IP Restrictions

Limit team access by location:
```
Office IP: 203.0.113.0/24
VPN Range: 198.51.100.0/24
Home Office: 192.0.2.1
```

### Two-Factor Authentication

Require 2FA for team members:
- **SMS Verification**
- **Authenticator Apps**
- **Email Confirmation**
- **Hardware Keys** (Enterprise)

### Session Management

Control team member sessions:
- **Session Timeout**: Auto-logout after inactivity
- **Concurrent Sessions**: Limit simultaneous logins
- **Device Management**: Track and manage logged-in devices

## Best Practices

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Team Member Can't Access Assessment" icon="lock">
    **Solution**: Check role permissions and department assignments
    1. Verify the member has appropriate role
    2. Confirm assessment is in their department
    3. Check for any IP restrictions
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Notifications Not Working" icon="bell-slash">
    **Solution**: Review notification settings
    1. Check user's notification preferences
    2. Verify email address is correct
    3. Look for emails in spam folder
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Collaboration Conflicts" icon="exclamation-triangle">
    **Solution**: Implement clear workflows
    1. Define assessment ownership clearly
    2. Use approval workflows for sensitive assessments
    3. Communicate changes in team channels
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
