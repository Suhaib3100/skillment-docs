## Overview

Effective communication is crucial for successful assessments. Skillment's email system handles everything from initial invitations to result notifications, ensuring participants stay informed throughout the assessment process.

## Assessment Invitations

## Personalization and Dynamic Content

### Variable Insertion

Use dynamic variables to personalize emails:

```handlebars
Subject: {{firstName}}, your {{assessmentTitle}} assessment awaits

Dear {{firstName}} {{lastName}},

You have been invited to complete the {{assessmentTitle}} assessment 
for the {{positionTitle}} role at {{companyName}}.

Assessment Details:
- Duration: {{estimatedDuration}}
- Questions: {{questionCount}} questions
- Deadline: {{formattedDeadline}}
- Time Zone: {{participantTimeZone}}

To begin your assessment, click here:
{{assessmentUrl}}

Technical Requirements:
{{#each technicalRequirements}}
- {{this}}
{{/each}}

If you have any questions, please contact our support team:
{{supportEmail}} | {{supportPhone}}

Best regards,
{{senderName}}
{{companyName}} Recruitment Team
```

### Conditional Content

Display different content based on participant data:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Role-Specific Content" icon="briefcase">
    **Customize by Position**
    ```handlebars
    {{#if positionType "engineering"}}
    This assessment focuses on coding skills and problem-solving abilities.
    {{else if positionType "marketing"}}
    This assessment evaluates creative thinking and analytical skills.
    {{else}}
    This assessment covers general aptitude and role-specific competencies.
    {{/if}}
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Experience Level Adaptation" icon="graduation-cap">
    **Adjust for Seniority**
    ```handlebars
    {{#if experienceLevel "entry"}}
    Don't worry if you're new to some concepts - we're looking for potential!
    {{else if experienceLevel "senior"}}
    We're excited to see your expertise and problem-solving approach.
    {{/if}}
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Geographic Customization" icon="globe">
    **Location-Based Content**
    ```handlebars
    {{#if region "EMEA"}}
    All times are shown in CET. The assessment is available 24/7.
    {{else if region "APAC"}}
    All times are shown in your local time zone.
    {{/if}}
    ```
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Low Open Rates" icon="envelope-open">
    **Improving Email Engagement**
    - Review subject lines for clarity and urgency
    - Check sender name and email reputation
    - Verify emails aren't going to spam folders
    - Test different send times and days
    - Personalize content more effectively
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="High Bounce Rates" icon="exclamation">
    **Delivery Issues**
    - Validate email addresses before sending
    - Clean up participant email lists regularly
    - Check domain reputation and authentication
    - Monitor blacklist status
    - Review email content for spam triggers
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Low Assessment Completion" icon="chart-line-down">
    **Conversion Problems**
    - Simplify email design and clear call-to-action
    - Provide clear instructions and expectations
    - Include technical support information
    - Follow up with non-respondents appropriately
    - Consider alternative communication channels
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Integration with External Systems

### CRM Integration

Connect email communications with your CRM:
- **Salesforce**: Sync email activities and responses
- **HubSpot**: Track email engagement in contact records
- **Custom CRM**: API integration for bidirectional data sync
- **Activity Logging**: Record all communications in participant profiles

### Marketing Automation

Integrate with marketing platforms:
- **Email Triggers**: Trigger follow-up campaigns based on assessment outcomes
- **Lead Scoring**: Update lead scores based on email engagement
- **Nurture Campaigns**: Add participants to appropriate nurture sequences
- **Analytics Integration**: Combine email metrics with broader marketing analytics

<Note>
**Email communication is often the first impression candidates have of your assessment process.** Invest time in creating clear, professional, and helpful communications that set appropriate expectations and provide excellent support.
</Note>
