## Managing Your Subscription

### View Current Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Billing Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Billing & Subscription** in your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Plan Details">
    See your current plan, usage, and renewal date
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Usage Metrics">
    Monitor your monthly usage against plan limits
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Upgrade or Downgrade

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upgrade Plan">
    **To upgrade your plan**:
    1. Click "Upgrade Plan" in billing settings
    2. Select your new plan
    3. Confirm billing details
    4. New features activate immediately
    5. Prorated charges appear on next bill
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Downgrade Plan">
    **To downgrade your plan**:
    1. Click "Change Plan" in billing settings
    2. Select lower tier plan
    3. Review feature limitations
    4. Confirm downgrade
    5. Changes take effect at next billing cycle
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Billing History

Access your complete billing history:
- **Invoice Downloads**: PDF invoices for accounting
- **Payment History**: All successful and failed payments
- **Usage Reports**: Detailed monthly usage breakdowns
- **Tax Documents**: 1099s and tax certificates

### Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
1. **Immediate Notification**: Email alert about failed payment
2. **Grace Period**: 7-day grace period to update payment
3. **Retry Attempts**: Automatic retry for temporary failures
4. **Account Suspension**: Features limited after grace period
5. **Reactivation**: Instant reactivation after successful payment

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans mid-billing cycle?" icon="question">
    Yes, you can upgrade anytime with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle to ensure uninterrupted service.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my plan limits?" icon="exclamation-triangle">
    You'll receive usage alerts at 75% and 90% capacity. Small overages have a grace period, but significant overages may require immediate upgrade.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?" icon="undo">
    We offer prorated refunds for downgrades and full refunds within 30 days for new subscriptions. Enterprise contracts have custom refund terms.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription?" icon="pause">
    Professional and Enterprise customers can pause subscriptions for up to 3 months. Data is preserved during the pause period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
