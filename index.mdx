## What is Skillment?

Skillment is a comprehensive online assessment platform designed to help organizations conduct secure, scalable, and insightful evaluations. Whether you're hiring new talent, training employees, or conducting educational assessments, Skillment provides all the tools you need.

## Getting Started

Ready to start using Skillment? Here's how to begin:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your organization account at [skillment.in](https://skillment.in/sign-up)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Organization">
    Configure your organization settings, team members, and assessment preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Assessment">
    Use our assessment creation wizard to build your first evaluation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Participants">
    Send assessment invitations and manage your participant pool
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor & Analyze">
    Track assessment progress and review detailed performance analytics
  </Step>
</Steps>
